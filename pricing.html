Everything you need to know about the product and billing.

AI tokens are a complex topic related to all AI apps, not just Bolt. You can learn more about tokens here. For Bolt specifically it is important to know that most token usage is related to syncing your project's file system to the AI: the larger the project, the more tokens are used per action. Our top priority is to increase the efficiency of token usage in Bolt. We continue to make improvements so that Bolt uses fewer tokens.
Teams provide a shared workspace for users to collaborate on Bolt projects. The subscription cost for Teams is per team member. Each paid team member receives a monthly token allotment based on the subscription tier. Tokens are not shared among team members. You can read more about Teams plans here.
Starting July 1, 2025, tokens from a paid subscription will roll over for one additional month, making them valid for up to two months in total. Please note that an active paid subscription is required to access any rolled over tokens. For more details, please refer to this article.
Yes, you can change your plan by clicking Manage current plan. This will take you to our Stripe billing portal where you can select Update subscription to change your plan type.
Yes, you can cancel at any time. Click Manage current plan to be taken into the cancellation process.
